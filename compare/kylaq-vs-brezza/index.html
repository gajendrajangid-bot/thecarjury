Buyer Questions

Frequently asked questions

Which should you buy: Skoda Kylaq or Maruti Brezza?

The Kylaq is the safer, sharper driver’s SUV with a stronger turbo engine; the Brezza is the easier long-term family choice for efficiency, comfort and support.

Which is better for performance, Skoda Kylaq or Maruti Brezza?

Kylaq is substantially stronger. 115 hp and 178 Nm turbo petrol is eager once on boost. 103 PS and 136 Nm is smooth but slow for overtakes when loaded.

Which is safer, Skoda Kylaq or Maruti Brezza?

Kylaq has the decisive evidence edge. 5-star Bharat NCAP, six airbags and strong structural feel. Good safety fundamentals, but the current review evidence is less comprehensive.

Which is more practical for a family?

Kylaq for luggage, Brezza for ownership ease. Largest boot in the segment; rear bench is best for two. More familiar family packaging with strong everyday usability.
