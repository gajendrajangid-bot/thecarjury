Choose the
Kia Syros
  • You regularly travel in the rear seat.
  • You want sliding, reclining and ventilated rear seats.
  • You value the triple-screen dashboard and larger boot flexibility.
  • You want Level 2 ADAS.
  • You accept polarising styling and softer highway manners.
Choose the
Kia Sonet
  • You drive yourself most of the time.
  • You want the sharper turbo-DCT experience.
  • You prefer conventional styling and easier dimensions.
  • You want better ride and handling balance.
  • You want to spend less without losing core Kia features.

Buyer Questions

Frequently asked questions

Which should you buy: Kia Syros or Kia Sonet?

The Syros spends its premium on rear-seat luxury and cabin technology; the Sonet is the better-driving, easier-to-place compact SUV for most owner-drivers.

Which is better for performance, Kia Syros or Kia Sonet?

Sonet is the sharper choice. Same broad petrol and diesel logic, but petrol DCT calibration can feel lethargic in town. Turbo-DCT is punchier and more responsive; diesel remains tractable.

Which is safer, Kia Syros or Kia Sonet?

Syros has the fuller assistance suite. Six airbags, Level 2 ADAS, 360 camera and a strong local build score. Six airbags, Level 1 ADAS and 360 camera with good cabin finish.

Which is more practical for a family?

Syros wins inside; Sonet wins in tight streets. Sliding second row opens 390 to 465 litres and creates limousine-like flexibility. Smaller outside and easier in town, but rear space is only adequate.
