What do BUY, WAIT and SKIP mean?

BUY means the jury would recommend it now. WAIT means it is good but a price cut, update or rival makes holding off smart. SKIP means a materially better option exists at the money.

How is the jury score calculated?

Each score aggregates every credible independent review of that model into a single 0-10 verdict, weighted for reviewer track record and recency. It is not our personal drive impression.
