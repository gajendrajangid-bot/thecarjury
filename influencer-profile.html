About this juror

Faisal Khan is one of India's most direct automotive voices on YouTube. Where many reviewers hedge, Faisal commits, his takes are plainspoken, evidence-backed, and deliberately free of manufacturer influence. He has built an audience by saying exactly what he thinks, even when it isn't what buyers (or manufacturers) want to hear.

His reviews tend to focus on the questions that matter to real buyers: reliability, value, everyday ownership cost, and build quality over time. Less press-launch polish, more long-term accountability.
