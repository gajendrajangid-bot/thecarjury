# Car Jury — Trusted Influencer Master List

## Selection Instructions (Apply to Every Car Review)

1. **Start with the top 20** from this list (by subscriber count)
2. For the **specific car being reviewed**, check which of those 20 have covered it — look at their engagement on that video
3. **Pick 5–8 creators** whose coverage of that car has the highest engagement rate
4. **Hard rule: never use a video with <2% engagement rate** — low engagement signals either paid/seeded content or audience mismatch
5. If fewer than 5 qualifying videos exist in the top 20, expand to the next tier down the list

These instructions apply to all future reviews on The Car Jury.
